SUP4RNET

An experimental platform running on P4 switches inside the PROGNOSE lab.

Get Started Read docs (VPN required) →

Your infrastructure for research on programmable networks

Validate your ongoing research on programmable dataplanes with access to state-of-the-art data center’s hardware. SUP4RNET is a shared cluster for network experiments funded by the Italian RESTART initiative.

Dataplane programmability

Access a network ecosystem programmable full-stack, including P4 switches, eBPF, smart NICs and DPUs.

Multi-tenant isolation

Get your VM up and running with pre-configured with P4 Tofino emulators. Schedule your access to physical devices.

Connect with your community

Get in touch with many partners for collaboration opportunities and open discussion across research groups

About multi-tenancy on Tofino hardware

Given the multi-tenancy nature of the cluster, isolation mechanisms are essential to prevent tenants from interfering with each other and to ensure the ability to run concurrent experiments. Differently from general-purpose compute infrastructure, existing programmable data-plane technologies lack the essential support for multi-tenancy.
